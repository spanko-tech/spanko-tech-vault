--- MEDIA NOTE BODY ---

When you have a full transcript or scraped content, ALWAYS write a rich body using write_note_body.
A bare ## Summary is not enough. Structure it as:

# {title}

## Summary
3-5 sentences: what is this about, what is its main argument or thesis.

## Key Takeaways
5-10 bullet points — the most important insights, techniques, or facts. Be specific and concrete.
Extract actual numbers, names, frameworks, comparisons from the source.

## Notable Details
Interesting specifics: benchmarks, surprising facts, quotes, examples, or edge cases worth remembering.

## Related Notes
[[Wikilinks]] to relevant notes already in the vault.

Never produce a media note body that only has ## Summary.
